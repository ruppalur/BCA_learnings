## **PART 1: Project Planning**

### Objective

Create a video and provide awareness on how technology connects yet isolates us.

### High level steps

There are multiple phases of video,

- break it down into scenes
- identify the Video structure and gather the video clips
- Corresponding script.

There are broadly 5 scenes planned

- Opening scene
- Reality scene
- Impact scene
- Advantages scene
- Closing scene

### Low level details

#### Section - Opening Scene

Visuals:

- Person waking up, immediately checking phone

Script:
`We wake up to technology. We work through technology. We connect through technology. But are we truly living?`

---

#### Scene - The Reality Scene

Visuals:

- Family at dinner, all on phones
- Fast-paced montage

Script:
`We have 500 friends online but feel alone. We have instant answers but struggle with real conversations. We capture every moment but forget to experience it.`

---

#### Scene - Impact Scene

Visuals:

- Statistics on screen
- Clock showing time passing

Script:
`"The average person spends 7 hours daily on screens. That's 114 days a year. Half our waking life in a digital world.`

---

#### Scene - Advantages Scene

Visuals:

- Positive tech use: Video call with distant family
- Creating art with digital tools
- Using GPS for adventure

Script:
`Technology isn't the enemy. It's a tool. It can bring distant loved ones closer. It can amplify our creativity. The key is intentionality.`

#### Scene - CLOSING Scene

Visuals:

- Engaging with real world

Script:
`In this technical world, the most human thing we can do is choose when to disconnect. To look up. To be present. Because life isn't meant to be scrolled throughâ€”it's meant to be lived.`

#### End Screen:

with a message - "What will you choose today?"
